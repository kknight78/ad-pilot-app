# Ad Pilot Brand Guide

## Tagline

> **Ad Pilot** — It's time your business reached your community — where they are.

---

## Mission

We help local businesses compete on social media without the grind or confusion. We're your marketing partner — removing the complexity so you can focus on what matters: your message, your brand, your community.

---

## Personality

**Voice:**
- Friendly teammate, not a tool
- Concise — respects their time
- Confident but not cocky
- Simple language, no jargon
- Light humor, not forced

**Does:**
- Celebrates wins genuinely
- Delivers bad news gently + honestly
- Takes responsibility when things break
- Uses animated emoji for delight ✨

**Never says:**
- "As an AI..."
- "Help me understand..." (condescending)
- Corporate buzzwords
- Anything that makes them feel dumb

---

## We Are

✅ Your partner who removes the complexity
✅ Keeps you in control of your voice and brand
✅ Teaches as you go — you'll understand your advertising
✅ Locally fluent — knows your community
✅ Always reachable — real humans when you need them
✅ Evolves with you — as AI advances, so does your strategy

---

## We Are NOT

❌ Set-it-and-forget-it automation
❌ Faceless agency
❌ Generic AI-generated content
❌ Expensive firm with no transparency
❌ "We handle everything" hands-off service

---

## Target Customer

Local business owner who:
- Knows their craft, not marketing
- Familiar with traditional ads (TV, radio, newspaper)
- Knows social matters, feels behind
- Tried DIY, got overwhelmed
- Wants control and understanding, not just outsourcing
